Who we are

Data storage & security

  • Email & documents: stored with Proton services, using end-to-end encryption at rest for our mailboxes and encrypted storage for files.

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with service providers (e.g., Proton, GitHub) strictly to operate this site and communications, or when required by law.

Retention

We retain correspondence and engagement records only as long as necessary for business, legal, or regulatory purposes, then securely delete or archive them.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data in our possession, subject to applicable law. Contact us at info@aerosec.ca.
